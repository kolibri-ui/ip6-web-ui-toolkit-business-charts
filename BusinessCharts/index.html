Chart à la carte

The Extension of the Kolibri Web UI Toolkit

Intro

Chart à la carte is the extension of the Kolibri Web UI Toolkit to build interactive charts. The goal is an "assortment of items" that simplify the development of web applications. Business applications often have a significant amount of clients. The lean and structured composition of the toolkit applies the Projector Pattern in a classical MVC architecture. This allows a quick and cost-effective modification of the "look and feel" of the business application.
